where to stay

The best way to really get a feel for Cape Cod is to rent a house with other wedding guests (airbnb.com or homeaway.com have some awesome listings!) or go the Bed & Breakfast route. But if a hotel room is what you're after, we have some recommendations for you below. Falmouth is not a huge town though so whatever option you're thinking of for lodging, please book early, especially because our wedding falls on Memorial Weekend.

holiday inn
