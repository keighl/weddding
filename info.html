getting here

from boston & logan airport

Coming from Boston, the fastest route into Falmouth is via I-93 and MA-3 headed South. That will bring you over the Sagamore Bridge... a fine bridge indeed.

from the west & nyc

Although you'll need to battle the epic and ever-present traffic of I-95 in Connecticut, your reward is an awesome wedding and being on Cape Cod in the middle of Striped Bass season. Take I-95, then I-195 in Providence, and finally take MA-25 over the Bourne Bridge.

no car? take a bus!

Peter Pan has bus service to both downtown Falmouth and Woods Hole. The buses run from Boston/Logan, NYC, and Providence. Yes, Peter Pan is a hilarious name for a bus company.

In Falmouth, you can get a taxi with Falmouth Taxi (508-548-3100). They will squire you about town in exchange for money.
